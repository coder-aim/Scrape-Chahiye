Our Mission

ABOUT US

Our mission is to keep as much electronic waste from ending up in local landfills as we can.

Our reduce program encourages the reuse and repair of electronics before recycling, extending their lifespan.

We provide easy access and incentives for communities to recycle more, helping preserve our environment.
